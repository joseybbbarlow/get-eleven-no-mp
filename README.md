## ğŸ® How It Works (Just Like Webkinz)

### Objective:
Clear all cards from the pyramid by making groups that sum to 11

### Gameplay:
1. **Click cards** that add up to 11
2. **They auto-remove** when sum = 11
3. **Draw from deck** adds cards BACK to pyramid
4. **Clear 5 groups without drawing** = Bonus card!
5. **Bonus cards** appear on the right side
6. **Use bonus cards** anytime to help reach 11
7. **Beat the timer** to advance to next level!

### Level Progression:
- **Level 1:** 148 seconds
- **Level 2:** 140 seconds  
- **Level 3:** 132 seconds
- Each level: -8 seconds
- **Minimum:** 60 seconds

### Bonus Cards:
- Earn by clearing 5 groups in a row WITHOUT drawing
- Random card value (1-10)
- Appears in BONUS panel on right
- Click to select, combine with pyramid cards
- **DISAPPEARS at end of each level!** (use it or lose it!)
- Can stack multiple bonus cards in one level
- Use multiple cards in one grouping

### Card Values:
- **Ace = 1**
- **2 through 10 = Face value**
- **NO Jack/11** - just 1-10!
